HOSPITAL NURSING REGULATIONS & LABOR LAWS
==========================================

1. SHIFT LIMITS
   - Maximum consecutive shifts: 3
   - Maximum hours per week: 40 (FullTime), 30 (PartTime), 20 (Casual)
   - Minimum rest period between shifts: 10 hours

2. COVERAGE REQUIREMENTS
   - At least one Senior nurse must be on duty for every shift
   - ICU shifts require ICU-certified nurses only
   - Emergency shifts require both ACLS and BLS certifications

3. FAIRNESS RULES
   - Weekend shifts should be distributed fairly across all nurses
   - Night shifts should rotate among eligible nurses
   - Preferences should be honored when possible

4. SPECIAL CONSIDERATIONS
   - Casual nurses have limited availability
   - Adhoc time-off requests take priority
   - Consecutive weekend work requires nurse consent
